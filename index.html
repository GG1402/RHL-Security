Legal Notices & Platform Policy

Operator: Rigo Think Tank — Rigo Foundation Incorporated (RFi). RTT Governance Officer: Ghani Gomuna.

Purpose: TANO is a civic accountability platform for community documentation, land justice, and governance transparency in Central Province, PNG. Operated under the mandate of RFi as a registered community organisation.

Legal Basis: Section 51 of the PNG Constitution (right of access to official information). Associations Incorporation Act (RFi registration). Customary Land Tenure Act 2026.

Moderation Policy: TANO reserves the right to remove any submission found to be false, defamatory, or in breach of PNG law including the Cybercrime Code Act 2016. All submitters declare their information is true and accurate at point of submission.

Data: All submissions are stored in a shared Supabase database. No personally sensitive data is collected beyond name and district. No data is sold or shared with third parties. Download activity is logged for accountability purposes.

Legal Review: Platform reviewed by Emmanuel Kila, Governance & Law Specialist, RTT Sector Specialist Register. June 2026.
